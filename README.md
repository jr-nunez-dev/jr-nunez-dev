👤 About Me
I'm Johnrex — an automation developer with a NOC engineering background. I spend my days keeping networks and systems healthy, and my nights (and a lot of coffee) turning the repetitive, manual parts of that job into self-running n8n workflows and AI agents.
My work sits at the intersection of two worlds:
🛰️ NOC Engineering — monitoring infrastructure, triaging incidents, keeping uptime high
🤖 Automation Development — building n8n workflows and AI agent swarms that turn alerts, tickets, and logs into automatic action
I'm currently deep in building an AI agent swarm (Telegram-driven, multi-agent orchestration) alongside a growing library of production n8n workflows that automate incident logging, ticket triage, and reporting for NOC operations.
